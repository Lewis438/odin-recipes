Pizza Recipe

Description

Even a novice cook can master the art of perfect pizza with our step-by-step guide. This homemade pizza recipe features a simple pizza dough and toppings.

Ingredients

  • For the base

    • 300g strong bread flour
    • 1 tsp instant yeast (from a sachet or tub)
    • 1 tsp salt
    • 1 tbsp olive oil, plus extra for drizzling

  • For the tomato sauce

    • 100ml passata
    • Handful of fresh basil or 1tsp dried
    • 1 garlic clove, crushed

  • For the topping

    • 125g ball mozzarella, sliced
    • Handful of grated or shaved parmesan (or vegetarian alternative)
    • Handful of cherry tomatoes, halved

  • To finish

    • Handful of basil leaves (optional)
